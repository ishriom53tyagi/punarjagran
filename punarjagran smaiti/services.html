For a civilized and socialized society, education is the only means. Its goal is to make an individual perfect. Every society gives importance to education because it is a panacea for all evils. Woman Empowerment
Punar Jagran Samiti is very conscious about women equality and gender justice, violence against women is increasing very where INDIA whether it’s in rural or urban. This violence also can see in domestic and public sector, violence against women both in the public and the domestic sphere is on the increase and most women get very little or no redressal to their problems on reaching a police station. It was felt that in the absence of any specialised training of the police or civil society institutions to handle the psychological and social needs of the victims, the women seeking assistance were further victimised.
