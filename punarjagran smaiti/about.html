punarJagran Samiti
When i see a old person starring food in garbage and he start approaching it continiouslyAfter my studies in Allahabad University, I came to Delhi for further education in 2004 CE. I did M.A., B.Ed. here and started preparing for competitive examinations.   In August 2014, I saw a 70 year old man outside Shivaji Stadium eating away from the trash. The despondent scene hit my conscience and goaded me as to why not work only for them! I suspended my studies midway and from the next day started teaching header_top_content beggar and street children. People’s support began coming for This work and in a short time the number of such learners increased. After this, a friend suggested me that it being a long-term work, an NGO needed to be established to run the affairs smoothly. Then I formed the “Punarjagaran Samiti” (literally, Renaissance Committee) in 2014.
