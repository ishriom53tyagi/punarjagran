Education
Education is makes an individual civilized, refined, cultured and knowledgable. For a civilized and socialized society, education is the only means. Its goal is to make an individual perfect. Every society gives importance to education because it is a panacea for all evils. It is the key to solve the various problems of life.
Women Empowerment
Punar Jagran Samiti is conscious about women equality and gender injustice. This violence can be seen in domestic and public spheres. It was felt that in the absence of any specialised training of the police or society institutions to handle the victims, the women seeking assistance are further victimised. PJS aims to resolve these issues.
Skill Development
A well educated population, adequately equipped with knowledge and skill is not only essential to support economic growth, but is also a pre-condition for growth to be inclusive since it is the educated and skilled people who stand to benefit most from the employment opportunities which growth provides..
